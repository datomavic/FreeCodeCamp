A brief timeline of Sir Tim Burners-Lee:

  • June 5, 1955 -
    Tim Berners-Lee is born.
  • May 20, 1973 -
    Graduates from Queens College at Oxford University with a degree in Physics
  • January 1, 1976 -
    Tim begins designing computer languages
  • January 1, 1990 -
    Produces first version of the world wide web.
  • August 6, 1991 -
    First website goes public while working at CERN.
  • January 1, 1994 -
    The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) is founded by Tim Berners-Lee
  • December 1, 2004 -
    Becomes professor at the University of Southampton, UK.
